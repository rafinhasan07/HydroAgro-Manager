Management Logic

HydroAgro Manager models a closed aquifer system. In this environment, the "Depth to Water Table" tracks the net cumulative change from seasonal inputs and extractions.

  • Sustainability: If the purple-blue line trends downward (increasing depth), pumping exceeds the total recharge.
  • Closed System: No natural baseflow exists to recover the water level; stability only occurs through manual balance.
